About

  Gachon University was selected as the first-tier university in 2015 by the government-funded SW-centered university. And this year, the first phase of the project was terminated and selected as the second phase of the university in a row.
   In response, Gachon University received 11 billion won in aid over six years. The benefits of field-oriented major education, global competency enhancement, sw basic/fusion education, and community sw education are available through the selection of SW-centered university industries.




 

Scholarship

  The 11 billion won grant is provided to students in the name of a scholarship to Sw-centered universities. Scholarships that students can receive include specialization projects scholarships, auxiliary research scholarships, internship scholarships, exemplary student scholarships, labor scholarships, and department scholarships.
