my data

With the introduction of my data, the ‘my data industry (credit information management business)’ also began to attract attention. This is because it became possible for a third party who received information from an individual to manage the data and analyze and recommend it based on it.

What would be good for my data business?

1. All your financial information in one place.

2. My credit and asset analysis becomes possible

3. You can receive customized financial products
